Key observations from this trace

  • Concept discovery is entirely KB-driven — there are no hardcoded verb lists anywhere.
  • _extract_intent_features returns mostly raw words/POS + language hints. The actual Concept objects are produced later by _map_features_to_initial_concepts (via ontology.find_concepts_matching). In the inspection test print, features['detected_concepts'] was still empty at that snapshot.
  • _resolve_dependencies is the heart of the new ontology system. It walks specializes, hasParameter, produces, and implementedBy relations to expand the 3 initial matches into the final 4 concrete concepts.
  • The final ExecNode tree is emitted via per-Concept Jinja-style templates stored in the ontology JSON files under kb/ontology/golang/.
  • Emission is still maturing (hence the placeholder lines above). The important part is that the correct concepts were discovered and wired together from natural language.
